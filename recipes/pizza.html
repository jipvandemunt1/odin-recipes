Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish enjoyed worldwide, known for its crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, and gooey melted cheese. Originating from Italy, pizza has evolved into countless variations, from the classic Margherita with fresh basil to meat-loaded options and veggie-packed delights. The dough, typically made from flour, water, yeast, and olive oil, is stretched thin or kept thick, depending on regional styles.

Once topped with ingredients like mozzarella, pepperoni, mushrooms, or olives, the pizza is baked in a high-temperature oven until golden and bubbly. Whether enjoyed as a quick snack or a shared meal with friends and family, pizza offers endless customization and flavors to suit any taste.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Sauce

  • 1 cup crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings

  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives (optional)
  • Fresh basil (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Shape the pizza
  4. Assemble the pizza
  5. Bake the pizza
  6. Serve and enjoy
